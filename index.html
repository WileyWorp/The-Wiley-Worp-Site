About Wiley

Wiley Worp is a self proclaimed content creator that makes youtube videos and programs websites. Wiley's specific age, grade, relationships, etc is not public information but there are things about those that if you ask him, he might answer. Things like whether or not he's in school, and other things that aren't personal and that probably won't dox him.

Wiley does not currently have a job but plans on it in the near future. He does freelance editing every once in a while and is hoping to do the same thing with programming websites soon. Right now he is working on this website, the one you're on right now, and a company named Worp Games, that has it's own "in-progress" website.

Wiley has one main philosophy, "Nothing will be bad forever." This refers to the fact that you will have times where you will feel hopeless. Think of that situation like a hill range. You feel like you are going up an endless hill, you think that there is nothing you can do to feel good. But then, something comes up, a beacon of hope. You start running down the hill, feeling better than ever. This shows one thing. Even if you feel like from now on your life will be terrible forever, you're just going up that hill again, and no matter what, you'll get to the top, and you'll go down, feeling as good as someone can feel.

Thank you for reading this, and have fun checking out this website.
